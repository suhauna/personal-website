Suhauna Hussain

Hi, my name is Suhauna. I’m a metro intern at the Tampa Bay Times.

This past summer, I worked at the Center for Public Integrity, a nonprofit investigative newsroom, as an intern on federal politics team covering money-in-politics.

I recently graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in political economy. While in Berkeley, I held several reporting and editing roles at Berkeley’s independent student-run newspaper, The Daily Californian. There, I covered the campus’s multimillion dollar deficit, high profile sexual harassment cases, contentious “free speech” events, black lives matter protests and tuition hikes.

I have also reported for the East Bay Express, an alternative weekly newspaper and provided analytical coverage of college campuses across the U.S. at The Chronicle of Higher Education.
